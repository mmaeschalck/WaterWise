About WaterWise

Did you know that the average American spends 8.2 minutes showering per day? Showering is the third largest source of household water use after toilets and clothes washers, yet is probably the easiest source to reduce, and help the environment. Simply reducing your daily shower time to 5 minutes over the course of a month for the average person could save up to 1000 litres of water!
